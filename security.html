SOC 2 Type II Certified

Annual SOC 2 Type II audits verify our security controls for data protection, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. Reports available under NDA.

  • Annual independent audits by Big 4 firms
  • Continuous control monitoring
  • Trust Services Criteria coverage
  • Report available under NDA for enterprise customers

End-to-End Encryption

AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. Voice data is encrypted before processing and can be configured for automatic deletion after analysis.

  • AES-256-GCM encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • Customer-managed encryption keys (CMEK)
  • Configurable data retention and auto-deletion

GDPR & HIPAA Compliance

Full GDPR data subject rights implementation, HIPAA BAA available, EU AI Act Article 52 transparency requirements met. Data residency options in US, EU, and APAC.

  • GDPR data subject access, portability, and erasure
  • HIPAA Business Associate Agreement
  • Data residency: US, EU (Frankfurt), APAC (Singapore)
  • Data Processing Agreement (DPA) included

Consent Management

Every call begins with clear AI disclosure and consent capture. Callers can opt out at any time. All consent records are immutably logged for audit trails.

  • Mandatory AI disclosure at call start
  • One-press opt-out at any time
  • Immutable consent audit logs
  • Configurable consent language per jurisdiction
