1 Introduction

Voxora AI, Inc. ("Voxora AI," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website (voxora.ai), use our voice AI platform, or interact with our services.

We operate in full compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), the EU Artificial Intelligence Act, and all applicable international data protection laws.

By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please discontinue use of our services.

2 Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, job title, phone number, and billing details when you create an account or request a demo.
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, and feedback you send us through contact forms, email, or customer support channels.
  • Payment Information: Credit card numbers, billing addresses, and transaction details processed through our PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment partner (Stripe). We never store full card numbers on our servers.

2.2 Voice & Call Data

  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings of calls handled by our voice AI agents, collected only with explicit consent and AI disclosure at the start of each call.
  • Transcriptions: Text transcriptions of voice interactions generated for quality assurance and service improvement.
  • Call Metadata: Call duration, timestamps, language detected, call outcome, and routing information.
  • Voice Biometric Data: We do not collect, store, or process voiceprints or voice biometric identifiers. Our accent adaptation uses anonymized, aggregate models only.

2.3 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and screen resolution.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, click patterns, and session duration on our website.
  • IP Address & Location: Approximate geographic location derived from your IP address. We do not collect precise GPS coordinates.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: See Section 10 for details.

2.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Racial or ethnic origin from voice analysis
  • Voiceprints or voice biometric identifiers
  • Political opinions, religious beliefs, or trade union membership
  • Genetic or biometric data for identification purposes
  • Data concerning health (except when explicitly provided by callers in healthcare contexts under HIPAA)

4 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): For voice recording, marketing communications, and cookie placement. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Contract Performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): For providing our services, managing your account, and processing payments.
  • Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): For tax compliance, fraud prevention, and responding to lawful government requests.
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): For service improvement, security monitoring, and bias detection — balanced against your privacy rights through regular impact assessments.

5 Voice Data & AI Processing

Given the nature of our voice AI platform, we take special care with voice data:

  • AI Disclosure: Every call handled by our AI agents begins with a clear disclosure that the caller is speaking with an AI. Callers can opt out and transfer to a human at any time.
  • No Voice Profiling: We never use voice characteristics (accent, dialect, speech patterns) to make discriminatory decisions about callers. Our bias mitigation framework actively prevents this.
  • Federated Learning: Accent adaptation improvements use federated learning — models are trained on-device without raw voice data leaving the processing environment.
  • Auto-Deletion: Voice recordings can be configured for automatic deletion after transcription, after a specified retention period, or immediately upon caller request.

6 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist with hosting (AWS/GCP), payment processing (Stripe), analytics, and customer support — all bound by Data Processing Agreements (DPAs).
  • Your CRM/Integrations: When you connect Voxora AI to your CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.), call data flows to those platforms per your configuration. You control what data is shared.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation — we will notify you unless legally prohibited from doing so.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred — you will be notified in advance and given the option to delete your data.
  • Bias Auditors: Anonymous, aggregated data may be shared with independent third-party auditors for quarterly bias assessments. No personally identifiable voice data is shared.

8 Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

Encryption

AES-256-GCM at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. Customer-managed encryption keys (CMEK) available for Enterprise plans.

Access Controls

Role-based access control (RBAC), multi-factor authentication (MFA), and principle of least privilege for all internal systems.

Monitoring

24/7 security monitoring, intrusion detection, and automated threat response. SOC 2 Type II certified with annual audits.

Incident Response

Documented incident response plan with 72-hour breach notification (GDPR) and immediate customer notification protocols.

9 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Residents)

  • •Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • •Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • •Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • •Restriction: Restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • •Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • •Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • •Automated Decisions: Right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling.
  • •Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • •Know: Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold.
  • •Delete: Right to request deletion of personal information.
  • •Opt-Out: Right to opt out of the sale of personal information. Note: Voxora AI does not sell personal data.
  • •Non-Discrimination: Right not to be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@voxora.ai or use the request form in your account dashboard. We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA).

13 HIPAA Notice

For healthcare clients, we function as a Business Associate under HIPAA. Additional protections include:

  • Business Associate Agreement (BAA) executed before any PHI processing
  • Protected Health Information (PHI) is encrypted and access-controlled at all times
  • Minimum necessary standard applied — only essential PHI is processed
  • Breach notification within 60 days per HIPAA Breach Notification Rule
  • Annual HIPAA security risk assessments conducted by qualified assessors

14 AI Transparency & EU AI Act

In compliance with the EU AI Act and our commitment to responsible AI:

  • Mandatory Disclosure: All callers are informed they are interacting with an AI system (Article 52 compliance).
  • Risk Classification: Our voice AI is classified and managed according to the EU AI Act risk framework.
  • Explainability: Callers can request explanations for AI decisions made during their interaction.
  • Human Oversight: Human agents can intervene in any AI interaction, and callers can request human transfer at any time.
  • Bias Documentation: We maintain public summary reports of our quarterly bias audits, available upon request.
